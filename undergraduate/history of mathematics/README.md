# A Mathematical Journey

This folder contains my personal notes and summaries from **A History of Mathematics: An Introduction** by Victor J. Katz. I am writing them in Persian (Farsi) as a personal notebook titled **"سفر ریاضی" (A Mathematical Journey)**.

The goal is not just to memorize dates or names, but to understand how mathematical ideas evolved and how they connect to the pure mathematics I am studying concurrently.
